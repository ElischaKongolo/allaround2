Returns & Exchanges

Easy returns and exchanges within 30 days. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Return Policy

Day 1-14
30-Day Return Window
You have 30 days from the delivery date to return your sneakers for a full refund. Items must be in original condition with tags attached.
Day 15-30
Restocking Fee (15%)
Returns between days 15-30 may incur a 15% restocking fee to cover processing costs.
After Day 30
No Returns Accepted
Unfortunately, we cannot accept returns after 30 days from delivery date.

Exchange Policy

Within 7 Days
Size Exchanges
Exchange for a different size within 7 days of delivery. Subject to availability.
Within 14 Days
Style Exchanges
Exchange for a different style within 14 days. Price difference may apply.
After 14 Days
No Exchanges
Exchanges not accepted after 14 days from delivery date.

Condition Requirements

  • Sneakers must be unworn and in original condition
  • Original box and packaging must be included
  • All tags must be attached and undamaged
  • No signs of wear, damage, or alteration
  • Proof of purchase required (receipt or order confirmation)
