About All Around

South Africa's trusted destination for authentic sneakers since 2020

Our Story

All Around Sneakers was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to bring the world's most sought-after sneakers to South African sneakerheads. What started as a passion project between friends has grown into one of the country's most trusted sneaker marketplaces.

We believe everyone deserves access to authentic, high-quality sneakers. That's why we've built a platform that connects verified sellers with passionate buyers, ensuring every pair that passes through our marketplace is 100% authentic.
