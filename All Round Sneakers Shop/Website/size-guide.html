Fitting Tips

Measure Your Feet

Measure your feet in the afternoon when they're slightly swollen. Stand on a piece of paper and trace both feet.

Consider Socks

Wear the type of socks you normally wear when trying on sneakers for accurate sizing.

Toe Room

Leave about a thumb's width (1.3cm) between your longest toe and the end of the shoe.

Walk Around

Take a few steps in the shoes to ensure they feel comfortable and don't slip.

Try Different Sizes

Sizing can vary between brands. Always check the specific brand's size chart.
