Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know sneakers are authentic?

Every sneaker sold on our platform goes through a rigorous authentication process by our team of experts. We guarantee 100% authentic products or your money back.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping within South Africa takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for an additional fee.

What is Your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn sneakers in original condition. Simply contact our support team to initiate a return.

How do I sell my sneakers?

Visit our Sellers Page to list your sneakers. Create an account, upload photos, set your price, and We'll handle the rest!
